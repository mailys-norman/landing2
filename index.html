Ayuda ESSEC Project

The launch of a new project

AYUDA ESSEC is an association that was created by ESSEC students. It's goal is to raise funds to help a struggling primary school in Mexico. The money raised will contribute to improving the school's infrastructure and permiting the creation of certain programs such as academic support and after school activities. As the concept is currently being developped, we are excited to see where this exciting project might take us!
